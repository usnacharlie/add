Common Questions

Do I need internet to register via USSD?

No, USSD works without internet. You only need a mobile phone with network coverage.

What information do I need?

You'll need: Full name, NRC number, date of birth, gender, and location details.

How much does it cost?

Registration is FREE. You only pay standard USSD charges (less than K0.20). Annual membership is K25.

How long does it take?

USSD registration takes 5-10 minutes depending on response time.

Will I get confirmation?

Yes! You'll receive an SMS with your membership number and next steps.

Can I register someone else?

No, each person must register themselves using their own phone for security.
