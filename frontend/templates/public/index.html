Wealth Creation Mindset

Job Creators, Not Job Seekers

Working with the UPND Government, ADD champions entrepreneurship as the path to prosperity. Jobs alone cannot solve Zambia's economic challenges—we need citizens who create wealth, build businesses, and generate employment opportunities. Together, we're transforming mindsets across all 10 provinces.

01

Entrepreneurial Mindset Development

ADD transforms job-seeking mentality into wealth-creating thinking. We train Zambians to identify business opportunities, develop innovative solutions, and build sustainable enterprises. In partnership with UPND Government initiatives, we're cultivating a generation of entrepreneurs who create jobs rather than seek them.

02

Business Mentorship & Wealth Creation

Successful entrepreneurs mentor aspiring business owners, sharing real-world experience in building wealth. Our members guide youth through business planning, market entry, scaling strategies, and wealth management—not how to write CVs, but how to create companies that hire others and generate lasting prosperity for Zambian families.

03

From Job Seekers to Employers

We facilitate practical business training that transforms job seekers into job creators. Through workshops, startup incubation, and hands-on entrepreneurship programs, ADD members learn to launch viable businesses that employ others. Working with UPND Government support schemes, we're building an ecosystem where Zambians create their own economic opportunities.

04

Capital Access & Investment Networks

Wealth creation requires capital. ADD, alongside UPND Government financial programs, connects entrepreneurs with funding sources—from microfinance to venture capital. We facilitate access to startup loans, grants, and investor networks so youth and women can launch businesses that generate wealth, not just pursue scarce employment opportunities.

05

Pro-Business Policy Advocacy

Partnering with the UPND Government, ADD advocates for policies that enable wealth creation— reducing business registration costs, simplifying tax compliance, streamlining licensing, and removing bureaucratic obstacles. We push for reforms that make it easier for Zambians to start and grow profitable businesses that create jobs and build generational wealth.

06

Market Access & Economic Opportunities

Beyond seeking employment, ADD members gain access to procurement opportunities, supplier networks, and market linkages. We connect entrepreneurs to government tenders, private sector contracts, and export markets. Through collective economic action, we create wealth distribution channels that benefit Zambian business owners across all provinces.

"Jobs alone cannot solve Zambia's economic challenges. We must shift from a job-seeking mentality to a wealth-creating mindset. ADD, working with the UPND Government, is empowering Zambians to become entrepreneurs, business owners, and job creators. When our youth build companies instead of searching for employment, we create sustainable prosperity for generations to come."

— Charles Milupi, Party President
