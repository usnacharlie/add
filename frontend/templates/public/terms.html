Terms of Service

Membership terms and conditions

1. Membership Eligibility

To become a member of the Alliance for Democracy and Development (ADD), you must:

  • Be a Zambian citizen aged 18 years or older
  • Provide accurate and complete registration information
  • Not be a member of any other political party
  • Support the vision and values of ADD and the UPND Alliance
  • Comply with the Constitution of Zambia and all applicable laws

2. Registration Process

Member registration can be completed through multiple channels:

  • Web Registration: Online at partymembership.zm
  • USSD Registration: Dial *388*3# from any mobile phone
  • Mobile App: Download our official ADD mobile application
  • Physical Registration: At designated ADD offices and events

All registrations are subject to verification and approval by ADD officials.

3. Member Responsibilities

As an ADD member, you agree to:

  • Uphold the party constitution and manifesto
  • Conduct yourself in a manner that reflects positively on ADD
  • Participate constructively in party activities and democratic processes
  • Respect fellow members and maintain party unity
  • Report any suspicious or illegal activities to party officials

4. Member Benefits

ADD membership provides access to:

  • Participation in party meetings, rallies, and events
  • Voting rights in internal party elections
  • Digital membership card with QR code verification
  • Regular updates on party activities and political developments
  • Volunteer opportunities and leadership development programs
  • Access to party resources and educational materials

5. Free Membership

ADD membership is completely free for all Zambian citizens:

  • No Registration Fees: Joining ADD costs nothing
  • No Annual Dues: No yearly membership fees required
  • Voluntary Contributions: Members may make optional donations to support party activities
  • Equal Access: All benefits available to every member regardless of financial contribution

Your participation and democratic engagement are valued above all else.

6. Code of Conduct

All members must adhere to the following code of conduct:

  • Respect for democratic principles and human rights
  • Non-violence in all political activities
  • Tolerance and respect for diversity
  • Honesty and integrity in all dealings
  • Confidentiality of sensitive party information
  • Compliance with Electoral Commission of Zambia (ECZ) regulations

7. Disciplinary Actions

Members who violate these terms may face disciplinary action including:

  • Warning: Formal notice of misconduct
  • Suspension: Temporary loss of membership privileges
  • Expulsion: Permanent removal from the party

All disciplinary actions follow due process and members have the right to appeal.

8. Privacy and Data Protection

We are committed to protecting your personal information:

  • Personal data is collected and used in accordance with our Privacy Policy
  • Information may be shared with ECZ for compliance purposes
  • Members can update their information at any time
  • Data security measures are implemented to protect against unauthorized access

9. Platform Usage

When using our digital platforms, members agree to:

  • Provide accurate and truthful information
  • Not share login credentials with others
  • Report technical issues or security concerns
  • Use platforms only for legitimate party-related activities
  • Not attempt to hack, disrupt, or misuse the systems

10. Termination

Membership may be terminated by:

  • Voluntary Resignation: Member chooses to leave the party
  • Expulsion: Party decision due to misconduct
  • Inactivity: No participation for extended periods
  • Dual Membership: Joining another political party

11. Disputes and Grievances

Members with disputes or grievances can:

  • Contact local party officials for initial resolution
  • Escalate to provincial or national leadership if necessary
  • Request mediation through party dispute resolution mechanisms
  • Appeal decisions through established party procedures

12. Changes to Terms

These terms may be updated from time to time:

  • Members will be notified of significant changes
  • Continued membership constitutes acceptance of new terms
  • Changes become effective 30 days after notification
  • Members who disagree with changes may terminate their membership

Contact Information

For questions about these terms or membership issues:

  • Email: legal@partymembership.zm
  • Phone: +260 211 123 456
  • Address: ADD Legal Department, Plot 123, Independence Avenue, Lusaka
  • USSD: Dial *388*3# for support options
