Privacy Policy

How we protect your personal information

Information We Collect

When you register as a member of the Alliance for Democracy and Development (ADD), we collect the following information:

  • Personal identification information (name, NRC number, date of birth)
  • Contact information (phone number, email address, postal address)
  • Demographic information (province, district, constituency, ward)
  • Educational background and occupation (for statistical purposes)
  • Language preferences and literacy level

How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used for the following purposes:

  • Membership registration and verification
  • Communication about party activities and events
  • Compliance with Electoral Commission of Zambia (ECZ) requirements
  • Statistical analysis for party planning and strategy
  • Provision of services in your preferred language
  • Mobile money payment processing for membership fees

Information Sharing

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • ECZ Compliance: As required by Zambian electoral law for political party registration
  • UPND Alliance: With our alliance partners for coordinated political activities
  • Service Providers: With third-party services (SMS, email, payment processors) necessary for platform operation
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or legal process

We will never: Sell your personal information to third parties or use it for commercial purposes unrelated to ADD activities.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encrypted data transmission and storage
  • Secure authentication systems
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls limiting who can view your information
  • Secure payment processing through certified providers

Your Rights

As an ADD member, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a common format
  • Objection: Object to certain types of data processing

Communication Preferences

You can control how we communicate with you:

  • Choose your preferred communication language
  • Select communication channels (SMS, email, phone calls)
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
  • Update your contact preferences at any time

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as:

  • You remain an active ADD member
  • Required by ECZ regulations for political party records
  • Necessary for legal compliance or dispute resolution

Inactive accounts may be archived after 2 years of inactivity, with notification sent to your registered contact information.

Children's Privacy

Our membership platform is designed for individuals aged 18 and above, in accordance with Zambian voting age requirements. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or wish to exercise your rights, contact us:

  • Email: privacy@partymembership.zm
  • Phone: +260 211 123 456
  • Address: ADD Headquarters, Plot 123, Independence Avenue, Lusaka
  • USSD: Dial *388*3# and follow privacy options

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through:

  • Email notification to registered members
  • SMS alerts for important changes
  • Website announcements
  • USSD notifications for USSD users
