UPND Alliance Partner

Building a democratic and prosperous Zambia through collaborative governance and sustainable development. As partners in the UPND Alliance, we are committed to delivering on our shared vision for Zambia.

Our Vision

A democratic and prosperous Zambia where every citizen enjoys equal opportunities for development and participates meaningfully in governance.

Our Mission

To promote democratic values, good governance, and sustainable development through inclusive politics, collaborative leadership, and citizen participation.

Our Policy Pillars

Democratic Governance

Strengthening Democracy
  • Constitutional reforms to enhance democratic institutions
  • Independent judiciary and separation of powers
  • Electoral reforms for free and fair elections
  • Decentralization of power to local communities
  • Transparent and accountable government
Citizen Participation
  • Enhanced civic education programs
  • Public participation in policy making
  • Digital platforms for citizen engagement
  • Youth and women participation in governance

Economic Development

Sustainable Growth
  • Economic diversification beyond mining
  • Support for small and medium enterprises
  • Infrastructure development programs
  • Agricultural modernization and value addition
  • Tourism industry development
Job Creation
  • Youth employment programs
  • Skills development and vocational training
  • Public-private partnerships
  • Innovation and technology hubs

Social Development

Healthcare
  • Universal healthcare coverage
  • Improved healthcare infrastructure
  • Prevention and public health programs
  • Mental health support services
Education
  • Quality education for all
  • Technical and vocational training
  • Higher education accessibility
  • Digital literacy programs
Social Protection
  • Social safety nets for vulnerable groups
  • Gender equality and women empowerment
  • Child protection services
  • Support for persons with disabilities

Environmental Sustainability

Climate Action
  • Renewable energy development
  • Climate change adaptation strategies
  • Carbon emission reduction programs
  • Sustainable transportation systems
Natural Resource Management
  • Forest conservation and reforestation
  • Water resource protection
  • Wildlife conservation programs
  • Sustainable mining practices
Waste Management
  • Waste reduction and recycling programs
  • Clean cities initiatives
  • Industrial waste management
  • Environmental education

Our Key Commitments

Collaborative Politics

Working across party lines to build consensus and deliver results for all Zambians.

Transparent Governance

Ensuring accountability, fighting corruption, and maintaining open government practices.

Innovation & Progress

Embracing technology and innovation to drive development and improve service delivery.

Long Term (3-5 Years)
